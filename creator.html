About the Designer

Hi, I'm Amandeep Chatterjee, the creator of this website dedicated to Tintern Abbey. As a passionate web designer and history enthusiast, I created this site to showcase the beauty and historical significance of Tintern Abbey in the Wye Valley. Through this project, I aimed to blend modern web design with rich historical storytelling, making the abbey accessible and engaging for visitors online.



I am a Class 8 student at St. Joseph’s College, Kolkata, India. I have a keen interest in web design and development, and I’m passionate about creating user-friendly, visually appealing websites.


If you’re looking for a dedicated and creative young web designer to help bring your ideas to life, feel free to reach out to me! You can contact me through the details provided on my Contact Page.


Looking forward to collaborating with you!
